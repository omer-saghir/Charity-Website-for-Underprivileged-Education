Futureroot Charity System

Our well-trained staff is providing quality education with a modern curriculum and interactive techniques.

High-Standard Schools

Futureroot School System is a pioneering network of schools strategically established in rural villages to deliver high-quality education to children who otherwise lack access to such opportunities. By ensuring financial sustainability, these schools are able to operate effectively without compromising educational standards, making quality education both accessible and affordable in underserved areas.

The academic curriculum at Futureroot is meticulously crafted to align with the standards set by top-tier private schools in Pakistan. This ensures that students receive a robust and competitive education, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to excel in their academic and personal lives.

What sets Futureroot apart is its commitment to a student-centered approach, where interactive and friendly classroom environments foster curiosity, creativity, and a genuine love for learning. The focus on exploration, collaboration, and critical thinking empowers students to actively engage with their studies, making learning a joyful and enriching experience. As a result, Futureroot not only educates but also inspires children to become ambitious, confident, and capable individuals, ready to take on future challenges.

Interactive Academics

At Futureroot, all subjects are introduced and reinforced through ICT so that our students can compete on all levels. Phonics, spoken skills, mental math, hands-on science, global knowledge, and civic sense are the basic subjects taught at all levels.

Rich Co-curricular Activities

Our students get time for extra-curricular activities as well, to make them excel in every field. Sports, art, field trips, music, and library make Futureroot schools a happy place for children.

Capacity-building for Teachers

In our year-long teacher training program, teachers are guided and mentored through professional development workshops and lessons. They are trained to teach interactively and engage young minds with knowledge.

Student Assessment Systems

Through a centralized assessment system, students are evaluated on a regular basis to track their progress. Results are analyzed, and corrective actions are taken to ensure that each child learns.

Use of Innovative Technology

  • Each school is provided with a computer and educational videos / resources to support the curriculum.
  • A special mobile app has been developed to take student and teacher attendance using a smartphone. Attendance is saved to a web-based database, and is available in real time!
  • A centralized school database is maintained, with up-to-date records on all the schools.
  • A system of picture-based reporting is in place. School staff fill out forms by hand, take a picture and send it to the Head Office electronically. There, trained staff enter the data in the database.
  • Computerized systems for audit and inventory are in place. Each and every item and rupee provided in the schools is accounted for.
  • Teachers are trained using face-to-face, online and mobile-based training options!
